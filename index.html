About Me and the Course

Personal introduction

Dayer C. Aniog loves playing online games with friends, but also wants to learn new things in life. He is a college student from Jose Rizal University with a program in IT. He is not fond of sports, especially races, because he easily gets tired. During the pandemic, his hobbies include playing games, eating, drawing, sleeping, and watching anime. Dayer is a kind, hardworking student, although he can sometimes be quick-tempered. He always thinks about the welfare of others and is grateful to his friends, parents, and everyone who helps him whenever he faces difficulties. He aims to make his family and friends happy every day. As he says, “Cherish every moment that you have with the people you love.”

Regarding his course, he admits that he does not enjoy logic subjects and finds them challenging. Due to frequent transport strikes, learning has sometimes been interrupted, making it even harder to keep up. He struggles to understand the functions of some queries, but despite these challenges, he continues to do his best to learn and improve. He hopes that the knowledge he gains will be useful for his skills and future.
